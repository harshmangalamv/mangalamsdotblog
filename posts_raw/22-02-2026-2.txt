Title: Ail Ail Aimshh
Date: 2026-02-22

This post is made in a way I can not imagine doing it myself using web and my skills. LLMs have really become the equalizers!

There is this thought experiment: 
Let's go back to the time when cameras were not a thing. To have a realistic portrait was a thing in its own. Those who were skilled in making them were the wanted ones. So were photographers in later times. But once the cameras reached to everyone, everyone became a photographer. This new technology was equalizer. It followed some real shifts.

LLMs being trained on such a huge data humanity have had are more like knowledge equalizers - far better than the search engines could. Now it takes a mere query to get the info one wants, aligned to the understanding level of the seeker. 

While the ethics of new technologies will always be debated, the real deciding force is not the technology, but the people who wield it.
